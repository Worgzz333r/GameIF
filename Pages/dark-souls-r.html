Dark Souls Remastered - Dark Souls re-release, developed for the PC, PS4, Xbox One and Nintendo Switch platforms. It was released on May 24, 2018.

Dark Souls is a challenging action RPG series by FromSoftware. Set in a dark fantasy world, players explore interconnected levels, battle monsters, and learn from frequent deaths

Gameplay

The player creates a character by choosing a gender, appearance, name and one of the classes (knight, mage, thief etc). Each class has unique characteristics and equipment. Defeating enemies, the player receives “souls” - currency for pumping and purchases. “Bonfires” - safe areas for recreation and pumping.

In Dark Souls, players face a cycle of death and rebirth. After death, souls remain at the place of death, creating an incentive to return and defeat enemies. However, dying again means they are lost forever. These mechanics form a unique gameplay experience based on caution and learning from mistakes.

World and plot

In Dark Souls mythology, the First Flame gave the world life, but its light is slowly fading. Players find themselves in an age of extinction, where civilizations are crumbling and monsters roam the ruins. Their task is to find a way to extend the Age of Fire or create a new order.

In the Dark Souls games, the player must defeat bosses and absorb their souls to become stronger. The finale always offers a difficult choice: continue the eternal cycle of suffering or try to interrupt it. In Dark Souls III, this cycle is shown as a source of endless evil.
