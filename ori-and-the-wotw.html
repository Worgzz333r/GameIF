Ori and the Will of the Wisps is a platform-adventure Metroidvania video game developed by Moon Studios and published by Xbox Game Studios. Announced during E3 2017, the title is a direct sequel to 2015's Ori and the Blind Forest, and was released in March 2020 for Windows and Xbox One.

Upon release, Ori and the Will of the Wisps received critical acclaim. Praise directed to the game included the story, characters, visuals, combat, elements of exploration, environments, chase sequences, and soundtrack.

Gameplay

Ori and the Will of the Wisps is a 2D Metroidvania platformer that emphasizes exploration, puzzle-solving, and backtracking. Players control Ori, a guardian spirit, as they navigate a vast, interconnected world. By acquiring new abilities and upgrades, players can unlock previously inaccessible areas. The game features a shard-based upgrade system inspired by Final Fantasy, allowing players to customize Ori's stats and attacks.

Will of the Wisps introduces new gameplay mechanics, such as Spirit Trials, timed challenges that encourage competitive play. Additionally, side quests from NPCs offer opportunities to earn rewards and expand the game's hub area.

Plot

Ori and the Will of the Wisps continues the story of the guardian spirit, Ori. After a joyful new beginning, tragedy strikes as Ori's beloved friend, Ku, is lost. Ori embarks on a perilous quest to save the corrupted world of Niwen. With the help of a wise old toad and a mysterious spirit guide, Ori must gather the scattered pieces of the Spirit Willow's light. This journey leads them through dangerous landscapes and forces them to make difficult choices. Ultimately, Ori must make a profound sacrifice to restore the world and ensure a brighter future.

After gathering the five wisps, Ori merges with the Golden Light, Seir, to restore Niwen. This ultimate sacrifice allows Ori to heal the land and revive their lost friend, Ku. As a new Spirit Tree begins to grow, Ori's journey comes full circle, symbolizing the eternal cycle of life and rebirth in the world of Niwen.
